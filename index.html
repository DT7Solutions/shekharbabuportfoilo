About Me

I'm Sekhara Babu

Pesala Soma Sekhara Babu has the vision to make Sikhara Nirmaan a pioneer in the construction industry and make quality construction materials affordable to everyone. He has more than 25 + years of professional experience in the construction industry and has dealt with more than 1000 dealers across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. He started his career with Tandava Krishna Agencies, established by his grandfather in 1994, and completed silver jubilee. He has a total of 100+ staff members under him. He completed his PGDCA Degree from ( JNTU-1994 ). He is also a member of the BNI Vijayawada business organization & Rotary MidTown Vijayawada, the World’s largest Club. Driven by his passion and humble by heart, Sekhara Babu is a man who believes that you should never be in a hurry in life but merely enjoy the ride.
