"Success is a Journey, not a Destination."

I embarked on my professional journey right after earning my PGDCA degree from JNTU (1994), joining Tandava Krishna Agencies, a legacy company founded by my grandfather. Leading the company through its silver jubilee, I have managed a dynamic team of over 100+ professionals and built strong relationships with more than 1,000+ dealers across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. With over 30 years in the construction industry, I bring a wealth of expertise and a broad network to every endeavour.

  • I’m proud to be an active member of the Rotary MidTown Vijayawada, which is the largest in the world. I take great pride in being an Ex-member of BNI Vijayawada.
  • I believe in savoring the journey of both life & business, ensuring that every step is taken with purpose & care.
